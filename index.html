§ Why BoringRate

Insurance shopping is broken. So we built something boring on purpose.

i.

One question, honestly answered.

"Who's likely cheapest for me?" — that's what shoppers want to know. We answer it using public rate filings and complaint data, not a 47-field form gated behind your phone number.

ii.

We don't sell your information.

Most aggregators sell each shopper to 3–5 buyers. You get hounded for weeks. We don't capture phone numbers, period. Email is optional and only used for what you ask us to send.

iii.

Methods you can read.

Every ranking links to its methodology. We tell you what data we used, what we couldn't get, and where you should be skeptical of our numbers. Trust isn't a logo — it's a paper trail.
