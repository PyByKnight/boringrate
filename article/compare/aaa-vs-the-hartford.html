Price: The Hartford vs. AAA

The Hartford averages around $1,632/yr nationally — about $888/yr less than AAA's $2,520/yr. The gap is widest for drivers 55+, where The Hartford's senior discounts (-14%) significantly outpace AAA's (-9%). The Hartford's AARP membership requirement is just $16/yr, a much lower barrier than AAA's $70–100/yr membership.

Complaint record: The Hartford edges AAA

The Hartford has an NAIC complaint ratio of 0.78 — 22% below the industry average. AAA/CSAA is at 0.87 — 13% below average. Both are good, but The Hartford pulls ahead. Its AARP-focused customer base tends to file fewer frivolous claims, which contributes to the cleaner ratio.

AAA roadside stays off your insurance record. AAA members file roadside calls (towing, lockouts, flat tires) through their membership — not their auto insurance policy. These events don’t generate claims or affect your rate at renewal. The Hartford’s roadside add-on, if available, files as an insurance claim.

Senior driver features: The Hartford is purpose-built

The Hartford's AARP partnership unlocks features not found in standard auto policies: a 12-month rate lock (your rate can't increase mid-policy period), RecoverCare (pays for help with household tasks if you're injured in an accident), and lifetime renewability (they can't non-renew you for claim frequency). For drivers 50+, these protections have real financial value. AAA doesn't offer equivalent senior-specific features.

About AAA / CSAA

CSAA Insurance Exchange is the insurance arm of AAA for members in roughly 23 western states. The key differentiator is the bundled AAA membership — roadside assistance covering any vehicle, travel discounts, and DMV services. Best for: existing AAA members in AAA territory who want to consolidate, and drivers with fair credit who benefit from more moderate credit pricing.

About The Hartford

Exclusive AARP partner with senior-focused policy features: rate lock, RecoverCare, and lifetime renewability. Strong complaint record. Best for: drivers 50+ who are AARP members — the combination of senior discounts and unique policy protections makes The Hartford the specialist choice.
